Jack Ma

The famed entrepreneur.

A prominent business figure, Ma is seen as a global ambassador for Chinese business and as such is frequently listed as one of the world's most powerful people by Forbes. He also serves as a role model for startup businesses.In 2017, he was ranked second in the annual "World's 50 Greatest Leaders" list by Fortune.
